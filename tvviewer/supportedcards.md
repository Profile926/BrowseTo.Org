## Known unsupported cards:  
- Dazzle DVC-170
- Medion 7133 TV Tuner
- Pinnacle DV500
- Pinnacle Studio 400

Note: Supported and unsupported cards are based on user reports. Please try the shareware version before purchasing the software.
